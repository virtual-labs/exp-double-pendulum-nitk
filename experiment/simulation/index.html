Objective

To study the oscillatory motion of a double pendulum.

Instructions

  1. Click the start button.
  2. The screen will display the note, click on ok to continue.
  3. The PLZT element is set to 45 degree in its holder with respect to incident light.
  4. The screen will display the next note click on ok.
  5. Set the polariser and analyzer at 0 degree and turn on the laser.
  6. The screen will display the note, click on ok to continue.
  7. Display the value of the voltage to 0V.
  8. Turn the analyser to 90 degree,clockwise.
  9. Adjust the voltage to 300V and Increase the voltage in steps of 25V,from 300V until 1000V and tabulate the measurements as shown on table1.
  10. Display the graph of vs V(volt).
  11. Display the graph of () of vs degree.Display the slope and Kerr Constant.
  12. Click on "Result Button" to display the results.
